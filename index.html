Bio

Folks that know his music don't even blink when they first hear Dave was born, guitar in hand, midway through his third composition. Of course, he comes by it honestly, his mother being a public defender and his father in the retirement home business. His earlier works deal with issues germane to the blossoming sense of self awareness that preoccupies adolescent consciousness. Examples of hit tracks from early on include songs like "Hot Boyz", "I've Got Friends All Over the World", and "Silly Little Boy" which explore the age-old cliches of Is he/she into me?, Do people think I'm cool?, and I'm being serious!, respectively.
